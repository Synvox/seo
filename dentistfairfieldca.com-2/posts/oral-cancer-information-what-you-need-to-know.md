![Oral cancer word cloud](/images/dentist-fairfield-oral-cancer-300x217.jpg) Oral cancer includes any type of cancer found in the oral cavity, including the gums, lips, and the lining of the mouth. Several risk factors contribute to its development, such as alcohol and tobacco use. However, many cases present themselves in people without these habits, but who have HPV, the virus that has also been linked to cervical cancer in women.

The full article is highly recommended, and can be found [here at the Seattle Cancer Care Alliance website](http://www.seattlecca.org/diseases/oral-cancer-facts.cfm).
